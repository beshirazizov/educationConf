Education: Classical and Modern Approaches

4-5th of April 2019

Baku, Azerbaijan

Department of Pedagogy of the Faculty of Social Sciences and Psychology of Baku State University (Azerbaijan, Baku), Faculty of Education of Charles University (Czech Republic, Prague) and The National Pedagogical Museum and Library of J.A.Komensky will organize an international conference on "Education: Classical and modern approaches" on 4-5th of April 2019 dedicated to a great Czech pedagogue, Jan Amos Komensky. The conference will be held in Baku(Azerbaijan). The aim of the conference is to review current trends and innovations in the field of didactics, to look for their development perspectives, to discuss controversial problems and to find solutions for them.
