X Republican scientific-practical conference carried out on May 08, 2018 in the theme of “History and modernity in Education” dedicated to the 95 years anniversary of nationwide leader Heydar Aliyev with the joint activity of the faculty of Social sciences and psychology, Pedagogical department of Baku State University, Ministry of Education of the Republic of Azerbaijan

The conference was organized and carried out by organization committee established with the chairmanship of A.M.Maharramov, the rector of BSU, academician of NASA at Baku State University (Z.Khalilov str.23, Baku). The representatives of university, educational and scientific institution, organization and department, also, state authorities and National Assembly from different cities and regions of the Republic were participated, 184 lectures were heard in the conference. Also, the citizens of foreign countries studying in our Republic – student, master degree students and doctoral students were participated in the conference (Turkey, Iran, Georgia, Cyprus and etc.). The conference was opened with the entry words of the rector of BSU, academician A.M.Maharramov, 3 lectures were heard in the meeting (prof. H.A.Alizada, prof. L.N.Gasimova, N.B.Imamverdiyeva and etc.). Education in the form of national-cultural values of young people in the heritage of nationwide leader Heydar Aliyev, the role of teaching and education in the formation of personality, teachers training, the main ideas of pedagogical classical and other issues were commented. There were 80 lectures and speeches in the II section (“Didactics. Teaching and education theory”). The modern problems of education, improvement methods of teaching quality, main targets of development strategy of education, monitoring and assessment system in education and other issues were reviewed in the lectures (prof. H.B.Bayramov, prof. H.H.Ahmadov, assoc. prof. M.A.Guliyeva and etc.). 60 lectures and speeches were heard in III section (“Education theory”). The development of theory in Azerbaijan, educational issues in people pedagogy, multiculturalism, tolerance education and other issues were enlightened in the lectures (prof. R.L.Huseynzada, prof. M.A.Ismikhanov, assoc.prof. E.A.Mollayeva and others). 10 lectures were heard in IV section (“Actual problems of high education”) and the lectures were conducted on improvement of profession-specificational training, development of new assessment means and methods, formation of creative capabilities of young students and other issues (prof. N.M.Mukhtarova, assoc. prof R.M.Mahmudova, assoc. prof. A.B.Hasanov and others). The discussions were carried out on the lectures in the “Round table” on each section after sectional meetings of the conference. The information of chiefs of sections was heard at the end of the conference, the best lectures were defined and they were recommended for publishing. The conference were concluded its work with the final words of A.Alizada, the dean of the faculty of Social sciences and psychology of BSU.

The conference was organized and held by the organizing committee of Baku State University (Baku. Z.Khalilov, str.23) that was established under the chairmanship of the academician of ANAS, the rector of Baku State University A.M.Maharramov. In the conference was participated delegates representing National Assembly, 22 universities, education and enterprises, organizations and administrations, government from various cities and regions, 157 reports were listened. Students, masters and doctoral students (Turkey, Iran, China XR, Nigeria and others) participated in the conference as well. The conference was opened by the rector of Baku State University, academician A.M.Maharramov, 3 reports were listened at the plenary session (prof. H.A.Alizadeh, prof. A.A.Aghayev, prof. L.Gasimova). In the reports, the philosophical, methodological and psychological issues of different pedagogical approaches were interpreted and commented on. The conference had lasted on 4 sections. 46 reports were listened in section first. In the reports (prof. A.A.Agayev, prof. H.H Ahmadov, prof.I.Jabrailov, prof. R.L.Huseynzadeh and others) - on different spheres of education and decency- philosophical pedagogical trends, training methods, educational concepts, organization forms etc. were reviewed. In the second section 49 reports were listened (They were about “Didactics” “Theory of Teaching and Education”). Innovative approach to training, modernization of education in the conditions of globalization, philosophy of assessment, stimulation of training, creation of supportive learning environment, etc. in the reports (Prof. L.N.Gasimova, Prof. R.F.Ibrahimbeyova, Prof. S.Askerov, Prof. H.Beyramov and others) problems have been commented on. In the third section ("Theory of Decency"), 31 lectures and speeches were heard. The theoretical-psychological issues of moral decency, ecological and aesthetic education of youth, multiculturalism and tolerance education, formation of national and moral values, etc. (Reports by Prof. A.N.Abbasov, Prof. H.A.Azizade, Prof. F.Mustafayeva b.) problems are explained. There were 32 reports in the fourth section ("The High School Pedagogy"). In the reports (prof. N.M. Mukhtarova, prof. M.A.Ismihanov, docent R.M.Mahmudova and others), the actual problems of the pedagogy of the higher school - the increase of students' activity and independence, the organization of work on the credit system in higher education, the formation of professional competence of students. After the conference sessions, discussions related to the reports according to each session were held around the roundtable. At the end of the conference the information of the department heads was listened and the best reports were noted. The conference was ended with the conclusion of the dean of the Faculty of Social Sciences and Psychology of Baku State University, professor H.A.Alizadeh.
